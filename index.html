Biography  

Ran Yi is an Associate Professor and Doctoral Supervisor with School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China. She is a member of Digital Media and Computer Vision Laboratory, working closely with Prof. Lizhuang Ma. She received her B.Eng and Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University, China, in 2016 and 2021 respectively. Her Ph.D advisor is Prof. Yong-Jin Liu, and the lab director is Prof. Shi-Min Hu. She also closely collaborates with Prof. Yu-Kun Lai, Prof. Paul L. Rosin and Prof. Ying He. Her research interests include computer vision, computer graphics and machine intelligence.

She has published 100 papers in IEEE TPAMI, ACM TOG, SIGGRAPH, CVPR and other important journals and conferences, including 59 CCF-A papers. She was selected for Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program by CAST, and won the Second Class of Beijing Science and Technology Progress Award, the First Prize of Higher Education Teaching Achievement Award of China Society of Image and Graphics, the Shi Qingyun Female Scientist Award of the Chinese Society of Image and Graphics (QingYing Group), the CCF-Tencent RhinoBird Fund Outstanding Project, the Dimitris N. Chorafas Foundation Young Researcher Award, the Top Ten Leading Scientific and Technological Achievements of the Big Data Expo, the ACM SIGAI China Rising Star Award, the Wu Wenjun Artificial Intelligence Excellent Doctoral Dissertation, the Excellent Award of China Industry University Research Cooperation Innovation Achievement Award, and the CCF-CV Academic Emerging Award, CAD/Graphics 2023 Best Paper Honorable Mention Award and Best Poster Award. She served as the Deputy Secretary General of CSIG Intelligent Graphics Committee and a reviewer for TPAMI, IJCV, SIGGRAPH, CVPR, etc.
